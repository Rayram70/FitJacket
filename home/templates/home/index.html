Our Mission

FitJacket is more than just a fitness app — it's a commitment to helping people stay consistent, motivated, and connected through every stage of their fitness journey.

Built for Lifters, Runners, and Everyone in Between

Whether you're training for a marathon, trying to hit a new PR, or just getting into a routine — FitJacket gives you the tools to plan, track, and reflect on your workouts.

Our platform is simple, intuitive, and designed to get out of your way — so you can stay focused on what matters: showing up.

Why We Exist

We created FitJacket because we saw a gap between performance and accountability. Spreadsheets and notebooks don't scale. Social platforms are too noisy. We wanted a focused, motivating space just for fitness.

If you're here, you're already ahead. Let’s go further together.
